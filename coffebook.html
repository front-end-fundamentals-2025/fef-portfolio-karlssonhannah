THE ART OF LESS

The Art of Minimalism is a personal project inspired by the work of British architect and designer John Pawson, whose minimalist approach to space, light, and materials has profoundly influenced modern design. Created as a tribute to his philosophy, this four-page coffee table book explores Pawson’s ability to transform simplicity into art. Through clean lines, balanced compositions, and restrained elegance, the book reflects Pawson’s aesthetic while also showcasing my own interpretation of his timeless style.
